Lot 10 Sports/Bracket Voodoo

NCAA Sports Analytics

Lot 10 Sports and Bracket Voodoo are two branches of one sports analytics consulting firm where Lot 10 focuses on NCAA football and Bracket Voodoo concentrates on NCAA basketball, especially March Madness. I interned with these companies as part of the MSDS program at USF, and I worked on projects for both arms of the company. You can find their full work on their websites, with links below.

  • Bracket Voodoo
  • Lot 10 Sports

Lot 10 Sports/Bracket Voodoo Blogs

  • Passing by Conventional Wisdom (Lot 10 Sports): An analysis of NCAA football coaches run/pass decisions by down, with a spotlight on the College Football Playoff teams plus a few more notable teams.
  • One Shining Model (Bracket Voodoo): A look into how three public NCAA basketball statistical rating systems performed in the 2017-18 season, including breakdowns by conference and home/away. Highlights the NCAA Tournament matches in the Sweet Sixteen.
  • Field Control Visualization (Lot 10 Sports): Visualization project showing how the field is split up between football teams during a game. More functionality to come on Lot 10 Sports' website, including ways to better highlight individual games and find specific teams.
